# Mutual Fund Chatbot: Product Naming Options

Here are a few options for the product name, categorized by the vibe they give off. Since this is a strictly factual, compliance-first bot for HDFC Mutual Funds (using Groww aesthetics), the name should inspire trust, clarity, and precision.

### Option 1: Professional & Trustworthy
*   **FundFact** (Simple, clear, emphasizes that it only gives facts)
*   **ClarifyMF** (Focuses on clearing up confusion around mutual funds)
*   **YieldSight** (Suggests providing visibility into financial data)
*   **NaviGuide** (A play on NAV - Net Asset Value, and guiding the user)

### Option 2: Modern & Tech-Forward
*   **AlphaDocs** (Alpha implies return/performance, Docs implies factual source material)
*   **FinLens** (Like a lens inspecting financial data)
*   **FundScope** (Microscope for mutual funds)
*   **GrowwSight** (If we are leaning heavily into the Groww ecosystem connection)

### Option 3: Action-Oriented
*   **FundCheck** (Implies verifying information)
*   **SchemeScan** (Scanning through scheme details)
*   **DataVest** (Data + Invest)

## Recommendation
I recommend **FundFact** or **FundScope**. They are punchy, easy to remember, and perfectly align with the "facts-only, no advice" constraint of the architecture.
